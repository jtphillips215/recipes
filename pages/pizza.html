Hompage

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings
    • Pepperoni
    • Olives
    • Peppers

Directions

  1. Brush edge of crust with olive oil and splinkle garlic powder on
  2. Add dollop of sauce to crust and spread thinly over crust
  3. Cover sauce with cheese
  4. Add toppings as desired
  5. Bake in oven
  6. Slice Pizza
